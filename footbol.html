Cristiano Ronaldo - Football Legend

Full Name: Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro
Date of Birth: February 5, 1985
Nationality: Portuguese
Position: Forward
Current Club: Al-Nassr (Saudi Pro League)
International Team: Portugal

Career Highlights:
• Sporting CP (2002–2003): Began his professional career in Portugal.
• Manchester United (2003–2009, 2021–2022): Won 3 Premier League titles, 1 Champions League, 1 Ballon d’Or.
• Real Madrid (2009–2018): Became club’s all-time top scorer, won 4 Champions League titles, 4 Ballon d’Or awards.
• Juventus (2018–2021): Won 2 Serie A titles, top scorer in 2020–21 season.
• Al-Nassr (2023–Present): Continues to score and break records.

International Career:
• Portugal’s all-time top scorer (over 120 goals).
• Won Euro 2016 & UEFA Nations League 2019.
• Holds record for most international goals in history.

Achievements & Records:
• 5 Ballon d’Or awards.
• Most goals in UEFA Champions League history.
• Over 850 career goals.

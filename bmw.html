BMW - The Ultimate Driving Machine

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) Founded: 1916 | Headquarters: Munich, Germany | CEO: Oliver Zipse BMW is a German multinational company known for manufacturing luxury vehicles and motorcycles. It is one of the most prestigious automakers in the world, competing with brands like Mercedes-Benz and Audi.

Key BMW Brands:
• BMW: Luxury and performance cars/motorcycles.
• MINI: Stylish compact cars.
• Rolls-Royce: Ultra-luxury cars.

Popular BMW Series:
• 3 Series – Compact luxury sedan with sporty handling.
• 5 Series – Mid-size luxury sedan with high-tech features.
• X Series – Premium SUVs like the X3, X5, and X7.
• M Series – High-performance sports cars (M3, M5, M8).
• i Series – Electric and hybrid models like i4 and iX.

The Kawasaki Z900 is a high-performance naked motorcycle known for its aggressive styling, powerful engine, and agile handling. Here are some key specifications and features of the Z900: Engine & Performance: • Engine Type: 948cc, inline-4, liquid-cooled • Power Output: Around 125 hp @ 9,500 rpm • Torque: Approximately 98.6 Nm @ 7,700 rpm • Transmission: 6-speed gearbox • Fuel System: Fuel injection with 36mm throttle bodies • Exhaust: 4-into-1 exhaust system Chassis & Suspension: • Frame: Trellis frame for lightweight and rigidity • Front Suspension: 41mm inverted forks, adjustable preload and rebound • Rear Suspension: Horizontal back-link, adjustable preload and rebound Brakes & Safety: • Front Brakes: Dual 300mm disc with radial-mounted calipers • Rear Brake: Single 250mm disc • ABS: Standard on most models • Traction Control: Kawasaki Traction Control (KTRC) with multiple modes Electronics & Features: • Riding Modes: Multiple riding modes for different conditions • TFT Display: Full-color digital instrument cluster with Bluetooth connectivity (on newer models) • LED Lighting: Full LED headlight, taillight, and indicators Dimensions & Weight: • Seat Height: Approx. 820mm • Weight: Around 212 kg (curb weight) • Fuel Tank Capacity: 17 liters The Kawasaki Z900 is well-regarded for its balance of power, agility, and comfort, making it a great choice for both city riding and spirited performance on open roads. It is a popular option among riders looking for a high-performance yet practical naked bike
